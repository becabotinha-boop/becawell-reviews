Who Should Approach With Caution

NuFace Mini is not universally suitable. You should consult a healthcare provider before use if any of the following apply:

  • You have a pacemaker or any implanted electronic device — microcurrent devices may interfere with electronic implants.
  • You are pregnant — as a precautionary measure, electrical stimulation devices are generally not recommended during pregnancy.
  • You have active acne, broken skin, or an acute skin condition — the device should not be used over irritated or compromised skin areas.
  • You have epilepsy or a history of seizures — consult your physician prior to use.
  • You've had recent facial surgery or fillers — allow adequate healing time and get medical clearance before introducing any device-based treatment.

Even outside these contraindications, results may vary based on skin sensitivity, hormonal factors, and baseline facial muscle tone.
