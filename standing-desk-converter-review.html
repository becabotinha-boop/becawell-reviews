How much weight can it hold — I have a 27-inch monitor and a laptop dock?

Most models in the $60–120 range are rated for 33–35 lbs on the upper platform. A 27-inch monitor typically weighs 8–12 lbs, a laptop dock 2–3 lbs, and a laptop 4–5 lbs.
