Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BecaWellness ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Pinterest integration application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize our App, we may access:

  • Your Pinterest account username and profile information
  • Your Pinterest boards (to save pins)
  • Permission to create and publish pins on your behalf

We do not collect passwords, financial information, or personal data beyond what Pinterest provides via its API.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Publish health and wellness content pins to your Pinterest boards
  • Manage your pin scheduling and posting frequency
  • Improve the performance of content we publish

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • As required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users
  • With Pinterest as required by their API terms of service

4. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time via Pinterest Settings > Apps.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke App access at any time via your Pinterest account settings
  • Request deletion of any data we hold about you
  • Contact us to update or correct your information

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Access tokens are stored locally and are never transmitted to third-party servers.
