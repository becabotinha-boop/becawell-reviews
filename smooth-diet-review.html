Common Questions

How long until it starts working?

The fiber expansion effect is immediate from the first dose. The hormonal satiety effects (GLP-1 support) typically require 2–3 weeks of consistent use to be noticeable. Most users report significantly reduced hunger signals by week 3.

Do I need to change my diet?

No dramatic changes required — but Smooth Diet works best with a mild calorie deficit. It suppresses hunger, which makes a deficit much easier to maintain. The formula does the heavy lifting on the satiety side; you just need to cooperate with a reasonable eating plan.

Is it safe to use long-term?

The ingredients are well-studied and considered safe for extended use. Glucomannan and psyllium husk are dietary fibers with long safety records. There are no stimulants that would cause tolerance or dependency. Always consult your doctor if you're on medications, as fiber can affect absorption timing.
