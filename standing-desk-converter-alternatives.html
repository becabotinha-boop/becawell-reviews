How much space do standing desk converters require, and will they fit my desk?

Most standing desk converters are designed to fit standard desk sizes ranging from 36 to 60 inches wide, though individual models vary. Consider that you'll need clearance above the desk for monitor height adjustment, typically requiring 24-30 inches of vertical space. If you have a smaller desk, look for compact models specifically designed for limited spaces. It's important to leave adequate working space around the converter for keyboard and mouse placement. Some models can be positioned off-center if your desk space is particularly limited. Individual setup requirements may vary based on your monitor size and keyboard placement preferences. Consider whether you have overhead obstructions like shelves or cabinets that might interfere with upward adjustment. Results may vary depending on your specific desk configuration and available space measurements.
