What to Know Before You Buy: Realistic Expectations

Let's be real about what a Sonic Facial Cleansing Brush actually does. These devices use high-frequency vibrations (typically 150-200 oscillations per minute) to gently exfoliate and cleanse your skin more effectively than manual washing. Results may vary depending on your current skin condition, consistency of use, and overall skincare routine.

Individual results vary significantly based on skin type. People with oily or combination skin tend to see more dramatic improvements in pore appearance and breakout reduction. Those with sensitive skin might need to start with lower intensity settings and gradually build tolerance. Some users report noticeable improvements within 2 weeks, while others need 6-8 weeks of regular use to see real changes.

The device itself isn't a miracle worker. It's a tool that enhances your existing skincare routine. Battery life typically ranges from 2-3 weeks per charge, depending on the model and usage frequency.
