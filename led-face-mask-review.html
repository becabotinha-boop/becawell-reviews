Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see actual results?

Most users see softer skin and a brighter complexion within 5-7 days, but true results (fewer breakouts, noticeably clearer skin) take 2-3 weeks of consistent use (4-5x weekly). Some people see faster results if they're also using supporting products like vitamin C serum. If you're not seeing anything after 4 weeks of daily use, your skin type might not respond as dramatically, which is rare but possible.

Will this work if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, start on level 1 intensity. Most sensitive skin types tolerate level 1 without irritation—it's gentle enough that some users with rosacea history report it feels soothing. If level 1 causes redness or burning, stop and consult your dermatologist. The silicone base is hypoallergenic and doesn't contain fragrance, so allergic reactions to the mask itself are uncommon.

Can I use this if I'm already on acne medication like Accutane or retinoids?

Check with your dermatologist first. If you're on Accutane, LED therapy might be too stimulating. If you're on topical retinoids, you can use the mask, but maybe reduce frequency to 2-3x weekly since your skin is already being stimulated. Don't use the mask the same night as retinoid application—alternate days instead.

How often do I really need to use it to maintain results?

Once you hit your desired result (usually after 3-4 weeks), 2-3x weekly maintains progress. If you skip it for more than 10-14 days, you'll notice congestion and dullness creeping back. It's not a one-time fix; it's a maintenance tool. Think of it like brushing your teeth—consistent use keeps results, inconsistency reverses them.

Is this better than professional LED facials?

Professional LED treatments are stronger and faster-acting, but they're also expensive and require appointments. This mask is 80% as effective if you're consistent, which most people find acceptable given the cost savings. It's a 'good enough and convenient' solution rather than 'best possible results.' If your skin needs urgent help, professional treatments accelerate it; this is better for maintenance and prevention.
