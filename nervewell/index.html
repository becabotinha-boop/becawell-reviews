What I Researched (And What Actually Has Evidence)

After being told my options were gabapentin (with its side effect profile) or learning to live with the pain, I went deep on the research. I spent 3 months looking at natural interventions with published evidence for neuropathic pain. Here's the honest summary.

🔬 B-Vitamin Complex

B1, B6, and B12 are essential for nerve repair and function. Deficiency — especially B12 — is a direct cause of neuropathy. Supplementing shows consistent benefit in multiple trials, particularly for diabetic neuropathy.

🌿 Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA)

The most researched supplement for neuropathic pain. 16 clinical trials. Shown to reduce burning, tingling, and numbness — especially in diabetic peripheral neuropathy. NerveFresh contains ALA at studied doses.

🧪 N-Acetyl Cysteine

Precursor to glutathione (the body's master antioxidant). Oxidative stress damages nerve tissue — NAC helps protect against this. Emerging evidence in neuropathy research.

🌱 Passionflower & Skullcap

Traditional herbs for nerve calming. Less RCT evidence than ALA, but consistent use in botanical medicine for nerve-related discomfort. NerveFresh includes both.

🥗 Anti-Inflammatory Diet

Inflammation drives nerve sensitization. An omega-3-rich, low-processed-food diet reduces inflammatory markers that worsen neuropathic pain — often dramatically.

🧘 Stress & Cortisol

Chronic stress amplifies pain signals via the nervous system. Cortisol reduction through sleep, meditation, or adaptogens consistently reduces pain perception scores.
