The Mechanism

The liver is the primary fat-metabolizing organ — it processes every gram of dietary fat and regulates fat storage throughout the body. When the liver becomes congested with toxins, excess fructose, or fatty deposits (non-alcoholic fatty liver disease affects 30% of adults), it processes fat 60-70% less efficiently. You can diet perfectly and still store fat if the liver is compromised. Mediterranean botanicals like silymarin, berberine, and artichoke extract have the strongest research support for liver cell regeneration.

Common Questions

How do I know if my liver is the problem?

Common signs: stubborn belly fat despite diet, fatigue after meals, right-side abdominal discomfort, slow metabolism. A liver function blood test (ALT/AST) is the definitive check — ask your doctor.

Is this safe with medications?

Silymarin and berberine can interact with some medications. Always disclose supplements to your doctor, especially if on diabetes or cholesterol medication.

Can I drink alcohol while doing a liver protocol?

Alcohol significantly slows liver recovery. Eliminating or drastically reducing alcohol during a liver support protocol is strongly recommended.
