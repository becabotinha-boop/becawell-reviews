The CDC's DPP Program: Start Here

Before any supplement, the most evidence-backed intervention for prediabetes is the CDC's Diabetes Prevention Program (DPP) — a structured lifestyle program that reduced progression to type 2 diabetes by 58% in the landmark trial (71% in adults over 60).

The core components: 7% body weight loss, 150 minutes of moderate activity per week, and dietary changes focused on fat reduction. It works. If you haven't looked into it, start there — it's the strongest intervention available, and it's lifestyle-only.

In the DPP trial, lifestyle changes outperformed metformin (the first-line diabetes medication) in preventing progression to type 2 diabetes — especially in older adults.
