Are these reviews independent?

Yes. We are not paid by supplement brands to write positive content. Pages include affiliate links — we may earn a commission if you purchase, but this does not change our editorial findings or scores.

How do you cover 108+ products across 24 niches?

Each review is built from ingredient research, publicly available clinical data, manufacturer disclosures, and user feedback patterns. We use a standardized 7-point framework applied consistently across every category — from supplements to online courses.

Do the reviews include guarantee or refund information?

Where the information is available on the vendor's side, yes. We summarize typical guarantee and refund terms so you know what protection you have before purchasing.

Is this medical advice?

No. Nothing on this site constitutes medical advice. Supplements are not substitutes for professional medical care. If you are considering a new supplement routine, consult a qualified healthcare professional first.

How often are reviews updated?

We review and update pages when product formulations change, new research becomes available, or pricing and guarantee terms shift. Pages are labeled with their review year.

Do you cover non-supplement products like courses and software?

Yes — our "Online Income" and "Crypto & Finance" categories cover digital courses and SaaS tools. The same framework applies: claims examined against what's actually delivered, with full affiliate disclosure.
