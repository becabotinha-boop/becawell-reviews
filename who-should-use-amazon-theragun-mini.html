Individuals with certain medical conditions — including blood clots, nerve damage, recent injuries, osteoporosis, or inflammatory conditions — should consult a healthcare professional before using any percussive device. The Theragun Mini is a wellness tool, not a medical treatment, and it is not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure any condition. If you have ongoing pain that hasn't been evaluated by a doctor, getting a proper assessment before relying on self-treatment tools is the more prudent step.
