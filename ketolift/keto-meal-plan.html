Average compliance: 83.7% — up from my previous generic plan attempts (31%). The difference: every meal in this plan used foods I actually like. I didn't feel like I was suffering through it. Days 2–4 were hard purely from keto flu, not from food boredom.

By day 5, energy returned and hunger between meals dropped noticeably. That's fat adaptation starting. Week 2 was significantly easier.
