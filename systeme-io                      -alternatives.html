Can I migrate from Systeme.io to one of the alternatives without losing my data?

Migration between marketing platforms is always possible but rarely seamless. For contacts and email lists, every platform on this list accepts CSV imports, so your subscriber data transfers cleanly — though you'll need to handle re-confirmation depending on your jurisdiction's email compliance rules. Funnels, landing pages, and course content are harder: these are typically built inside proprietary editors and don't export to a portable format. You'll likely need to rebuild pages manually in your new platform. Automation sequences can usually be reconstructed by exporting your logic as documentation and rebuilding in the new tool. Plan for this migration to take one to two weeks of focused work for a moderately complex setup. Individual results vary based on how much content you've built and how technically confident you are with the destination platform.
