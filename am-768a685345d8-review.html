Frequently Asked Questions

Will the compartments fit a standard kitchen drawer without modification?

The base system fits drawers 12 inches wide and deeper. Most standard kitchen drawers (typically 18–24 inches deep, 12–30 inches wide) accommodate it well. The modular dividers let you fill narrower drawers by simply using fewer columns. Measure your drawer depth before ordering — the product page lists exact compatible dimensions, and the 4-inch depth variant is the more versatile choice for kitchen use.

How long does initial setup actually take?

Budget 90 minutes for a thorough setup across three to four drawers. That includes the planning phase — laying out your items on a flat surface and deciding on zones before snapping dividers in place. Buyers who skip planning and assemble directly report spending another 30 minutes reconfiguring. The setup investment is front-loaded but doesn't repeat; once your layout is dialed in, maintenance is just returning items to their section.

Does the matte finish stay white, or does it yellow over time?

The finish does show dust more than a glossy surface would, but wipes clean in seconds without leaving streaks.

Can I use this in a bathroom vanity drawer with beauty products?

Yes — this is actually one of the strongest use cases. The 2-inch compartments fit lip products, mascara, and single eye shadow palettes; the 4-inch sections handle foundation bottles upright. The premium here buys a unified design language across your whole home rather than a collection of compromises.
