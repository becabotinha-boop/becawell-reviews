BecaWell is an independent product review and research website. We do not sell or ship physical products. All products linked from our site are sold directly by third-party vendors and manufacturers.

ClickBank Products — 60-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Most products we review are sold through ClickBank, which offers a standard 60-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. This means:

  • You have 60 days from the date of purchase to request a full refund
  • No questions asked — you do not need to return the physical product in most cases
  • Refunds are processed by ClickBank directly

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a ClickBank product:

  1. Visit clkbank.com
  2. Enter your order details to locate your purchase
  3. Select "Request a Refund" and follow the prompts
  4. Alternatively, contact ClickBank support directly
