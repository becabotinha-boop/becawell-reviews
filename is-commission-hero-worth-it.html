Is Commission Hero Worth It? An Honest Assessment

Short answer: Commission Hero can be worth it for the right type of person — specifically, beginners who want a structured, step-by-step system for affiliate marketing with Facebook Ads — but results may vary significantly, and it is not a guaranteed path to income. Whether the investment pays off depends heavily on your budget, commitment level, and willingness to spend on paid traffic in addition to the course cost itself.

What Is Commission Hero?

Commission Hero is an affiliate marketing training course created by Robby Blanchard, who claims to be one of ClickBank's top affiliates. The program teaches a model centered around promoting ClickBank offers using Facebook and Instagram paid ads. It is primarily a paid traffic strategy — meaning you will need an advertising budget on top of the course fee to implement what is taught.

The course covers finding offers, building simple landing pages, writing ad copy, and scaling campaigns that convert. It is not an SEO or organic content course. If you are looking for free traffic methods, this course may not align with your goals.

What You Get

  • Core video training modules covering offer selection, ad setup, and funnel building
  • Access to a private Facebook community for support and accountability
  • Done-for-you landing page templates designed to reduce technical friction
  • Coaching calls and live Q&A sessions (availability may vary by enrollment tier)
  • Ad copy examples and swipe files to use as starting points

The material is generally considered beginner-friendly, though some users report that the community support quality can be inconsistent. Your individual experience may differ.

The Case For: When Commission Hero Could Be Worth It

Commission Hero tends to receive more positive feedback from students who come in with realistic expectations and a realistic budget. Some scenarios where it may be a worthwhile investment include:

You Have Startup Capital

Because the model relies on paid Facebook Ads, you realistically need $500–$1,500 or more in ad spend budget available beyond the course cost itself. Students who enter with insufficient ad budget often stall before seeing any results. If you have the capital to test and iterate on campaigns, the structured approach taught in the course could accelerate your learning curve compared to figuring it out alone.

You Want a Defined Framework

For complete beginners who feel overwhelmed by the sheer volume of affiliate marketing advice online, having a single, opinionated system to follow can reduce decision fatigue. Commission Hero provides that structure. Whether that structure produces results for you specifically will depend on how well you execute and how the market responds to your campaigns — results may vary widely.

You Are Comfortable With Digital Advertising

Students with any prior experience running Facebook or Instagram ads tend to report a smoother path through the material and faster iteration cycles. If paid ads are entirely new to you, expect a steeper real-world learning curve than the course content alone suggests.

The Case Against: Reasons to Be Cautious

Commission Hero is not without meaningful criticism, and it would be misleading to present it as a straightforward win for everyone.

Total Cost Is Higher Than the Headline Price

The sticker price of the course does not include your ad spend, which is a recurring operational cost. It also does not include tools like ClickFunnels or similar landing page builders, which may be recommended or required. The all-in cost in your first few months could be substantially higher than the course fee alone. Plan your budget carefully before committing.

Facebook Ad Policies Create Real Risk

Many ClickBank health and financial offers operate in niches that Facebook scrutinizes closely. Ad account bans and disapprovals are a documented and recurring challenge for students in this program. If your account is suspended, your campaigns halt entirely. This is an operational risk that results may vary in how severely it impacts any individual student.

Income Claims Should Be Viewed Skeptically

Promotional materials for courses like this often feature exceptional earner results. These do not represent typical outcomes. The majority of people who purchase affiliate marketing courses do not achieve significant income, and some lose money on ad spend before finding a profitable campaign — if they do at all. Qualifying language is important here: past performance of other students does not predict your results.

The Niche and Offers Change

ClickBank offers cycle in and out of profitability. An offer that was converting well when the course was filmed may perform differently by the time you are running ads to it. Staying current requires ongoing effort and adaptability that goes beyond what any static course can teach.

Who Is Most Likely to Get Value From It

Based on available feedback, the students who tend to report the best outcomes share a few common traits: they have $1,000+ in available ad budget, they treat it as a business rather than a passive income shortcut, they are willing to test and iterate systematically, and they engage actively with the community for support when campaigns underperform.

If that profile does not match your current situation, it may be worth waiting until it does — or exploring organic traffic methods first to build foundational knowledge at lower financial risk.

Final Verdict

Commission Hero is a legitimate training program with a clear methodology. For the right student with adequate budget and realistic expectations, it may provide a workable roadmap into affiliate marketing via paid traffic. However, it is not a guaranteed income system, and results may vary considerably from person to person. No course can substitute for execution, testing, and willingness to absorb initial losses while learning. Weigh the total cost honestly against your current financial situation before deciding.
