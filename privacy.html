Privacy Policy

How BecaWell Reviews collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: January 2026  |  Effective: January 1, 2026  |  Operator: Rebeca Lopes

Overview

BecaWell Reviews ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our site, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information to improve the site experience:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, browser type, and device type — collected via Google Analytics (anonymised).
  • Cookies: Analytics cookies from Google Analytics (GA4). You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.
  • Voluntary contact: If you email us, we retain your message to respond to your enquiry.

We do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information directly. We do not run email lists or sell any data.

How We Use Information

  • To understand which content is most helpful to readers
  • To improve site navigation and page quality
  • To respond to direct enquiries

We do not use your data for advertising targeting, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond improving this site.

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services that may collect data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Anonymised traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Associates: If you click an Amazon affiliate link, Amazon may place cookies. Amazon Privacy Policy
  • AliExpress Affiliate: Clicking AliExpress links may result in tracking cookies from AliExpress/Alibaba.
  • ClickBank: Some links go to ClickBank vendor pages which have their own tracking.
  • Pinterest: This site is verified on Pinterest. Pinterest may track visits for analytics purposes.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies only. No advertising cookies are set by BecaWell directly. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Most browsers accept cookies by default — refer to your browser's help section for instructions on disabling them.

Data Retention

Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months. Direct email enquiries are retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request, then deleted.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including access to your data, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page when changes are made. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at hello@becawell.com.
